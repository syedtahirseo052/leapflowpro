❓ FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Is this a real Google Maps scraper?+
The platform uses a simulation in this HTML demo. The actual production version uses a Python + Playwright backend that runs a headless browser to extract real Google Maps data. The scoring, city coverage, and pitch engine are fully functional.
How is WhatsApp detection done?+
WhatsApp detection works by checking whether a phone number is registered on WhatsApp using the wa.me endpoint or WhatsApp Business API verification. In the demo, status is simulated. Real detection is available in paid plans.
What does the lead score mean?+
Scores are calculated from: inbox saturation in the target market, agency openness to cold outreach, digital maturity of the business, and whether they have gaps like no website or few reviews. Higher = better outreach opportunity.
Do I need a credit card for the free plan?+
No. The free plan includes 10 searches per day (300/month) with Google Maps Scraper and 3 pitch templates. No credit card required. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard.
Which countries have the best outreach results?+
Based on our research: Norway (68/100), Denmark (66/100), Senegal (67/100), Tanzania (62/100), and Rwanda (64/100) have the lowest inbox saturation and highest agency openness. USA (28/100) and India (22/100) are the hardest markets.
When are LinkedIn and Clutch scrapers coming?+
LinkedIn Extractor launches Q2 2025. Clutch Scraper and Directory Scraper both launch Q3 2025. Professional plan users get early access. Sign up to be notified when they go live.
