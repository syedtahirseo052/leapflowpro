📊 Scoring Methodology (transparent):
Country scores are calculated from: cold outreach industry benchmarks (average reply rates by region), digital agency market density (number of agencies per 100k population), and B2B inbox saturation (how many agencies receive similar pitches weekly). City scores apply a ±modifier to the country base (capital cities get −5 to −8, secondary cities get +4 to +8).

⚠️ WhatsApp Detection: In this demo, WA status is simulated based on country phone number format compatibility. Real detection requires calling wa.me/{number} and checking HTTP response, or using WhatsApp Business API verification.
